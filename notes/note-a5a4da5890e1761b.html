🇺🇸 BIS extends ECCN 3A090.a approved IC designer application deadline from April 13, 2026 to December 31, 2026

📅 Published 14 July 2026 · high confidence

What changed

  • BIS has amended Note 1 to ECCN 3A090.a in paragraphs a.(2) and (3) by removing the date 'April 13, 2026' wherever it occurs and replacing it with 'December 31, 2026' [FR Doc. 2026-06851, Supplement No. 1 to Part 774, ECCN 3A090 amendment].
  • The triggering date for authorized IC designer status and the submission deadline for applications to become an approved IC designer are both extended by approximately eight months [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUMMARY].
  • Applicants who submit by December 31, 2026 may be considered authorized IC designers for 180 days after that date [FR Doc. 2026-06851, DATES].
  • BIS estimates the extension will result in approximately 20 additional approved IC designers and an increase of 385 burden hours under OMB Control Number 0694-0137 [FR Doc. 2026-06851, Rulemaking Requirements §2].
  • BIS also estimates approximately 75 additional advisory opinions and 38 additional burden hours under OMB Control Number 0694-0088 [FR Doc. 2026-06851, Rulemaking Requirements §2].

Who is affected

  • Front-end fabricators and OSAT companies seeking to export, reexport, or transfer (in-country) certain applicable advanced logic integrated circuits under ECCN 3A090.a are affected [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION].
  • Entities that have submitted or intend to submit requests to BIS to be listed as approved IC designers, subject to End-user Review Committee (ERC) approval, are affected [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION].
  • Entities seeking to qualify as authorized IC designers under the criteria in Note 1 to 3A090.a are affected [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION].

Licence posture — The rule modifies the date-based triggering mechanism within Note 1 to ECCN 3A090.a, which provides a means to overcome the presumption applicable to front-end fabricators and OSAT companies under 15 CFR 742.6(a)(6)(iii)(A) [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION]. Entities that apply by December 31, 2026 may be considered authorized IC designers for 180 days after that date [FR Doc. 2026-06851, DATES]. The source does not state a general licence posture change beyond the date extension described [FR Doc. 2026-06851, SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION].

Effective — Effective date of rule: April 7, 2026; Extended application deadline (approved IC designer): December 31, 2026; Authorized IC designer status period: 180 days following December 31, 2026 for timely applicants

Instruments — 15 CFR Part 774, Supplement No. 1 (Commerce Control List), ECCN 3A090.a, Note 1, paragraphs a.(2) and (3)

Action items

  • EU-based IC designers or entities involved in the supply chain for ECCN 3A090.a items who seek approved IC designer status should submit applications to BIS by December 31, 2026.
  • Review Note 1 to ECCN 3A090.a paragraphs a.(2) and (3) to confirm updated date references in compliance procedures.
  • Assess whether front-end fabricator or OSAT operations involving ECCN 3A090.a items require reliance on the approved or authorized IC designer pathway and update timelines accordingly.
